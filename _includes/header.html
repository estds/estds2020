About

Water Security and Sustainability Forum is one of the ESTDS Forums sponsored by the Chinese Academy of Engineering. It has a clear focus on availability and sustainable management of water resources, and aims to bring forward open, frank, productive and multi-disciplinary discusssions. It is held every 3 years in Nanjing, China, where the ancient Qinhuai River of romance meets the mighty Yangtze. [Read on...]
