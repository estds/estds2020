  • Use of Cookies

    Cookies are small bits of information about your browsing activity that we store on your device. These bits of information allow us to provide you with better experience while you use our website. We only use strictly necessary Cookies on our website. These cookies are used to store your preferences (for example, remembering the message you have choose to hide), and create strictly necessary caches that could be used when you are offline.

    On our abstract submission page, we will be using additional cookies to facilitate your submission. These cookies would be essential for submitting your abstract.These cookies are from the The International Joint Research Center of Water Science and Engineering who is generously allowing us to use their dynamic forms.

    We don't use any Cookies for the purpose of tracking or advertising.

    You will see a message reminding you that we are using Cookies when you visit our website. This message will hide itself after 10 seconds if you don't do anything with it. It will show up again when you refresh the current page or go to another page of ours. If you don't want to be reminded again, please allow us to use Cookies by clicking the button in the message.
