The Academic Committee (AC) oversees all academic affairs of the Forum, including decisions of accepting abstracts for presentations, final programmes of events, and academic awards at the Forum. AC members will be requested to review abstracts (at a maximum of 10 abstracts) in their field of expertise based on their availability. The Secretariat will make such requests based on necessity.
